Hide

(Adapted from a poem I wrote in 2014)

How many times must I do this till its over?

Till I'm older?

The moon rises and falls, again and again--

Does my life have to be this monotonous way?


Before sunrays hit my head,

I climb out of my bed.

I'd sit silent on a chair.

For my future, I must prepare.


Numbers rule the world over here.

They say you need them for a career.

People keep telling me to work hard,

My playful wishes I must discard.


I must climb to a high place

Because life is a fierce chase.

The days are dark, but nights are lit bright.

I'd go to school in the day, and shed tears at night.


I climb, sniffling, into the depths of my heart

Where nothing can take me apart;

Where I lock myself in with all that I feel;

Where my dreams and desires must be concealed.

Dream

In the depths of my mind, I am alone.

I can create a world of my own!

Here I soar to my heart's delight,

No one can stop me, day or night.
